Welcome to FBI SA

Welcome to Freedom Bright Impairment SA, a community-led organisation dedicated to the development and support of individuals with disabilities, orphans, and youth in need. Since our founding, we have been working tirelessly to uplift and support our community.

At FBI SA, we believe that every individual has the potential to thrive. By understanding the ever-changing needs of our community and engaging with various stakeholders, we create avenues for empowering vulnerable populations and enabling them to take control of their own lives.

Water Scarcity

A critical need — our response

The community faces a critical need for water infrastructure, as water scarcity is a significant challenge in rural areas. Residents are currently relying on nearby rivers for water access. In 2020, FBI SA successfully established a site to provide some community members with access to water.

We are pleased to partner with Jam Water Service, which has helped us implement boreholes and an effective water purification system to deliver safe, healthy water to the community.
