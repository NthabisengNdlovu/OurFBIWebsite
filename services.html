Services That Support Our Community

At FBI SA, we offer a range of programs that support children, young people, and families. Our services focus on education, care, skills development, and access to essential resources that help communities grow stronger and more self-reliant.

What We Do

Comprehensive Community Support

From early childhood development to youth employment, we provide holistic services that address the real needs of our community.

Disability Support

We recognized the critical lack of early childhood development (ECD) options for children with disabilities. Many parents cannot afford special schools requiring specialized care.

In response, we launched FBI SA Day Care for children with exceptional abilities. Our staff receive regular training to ensure they can effectively support every child, regardless of their disability.

  • Specialized ECD programs
  • Regular staff training
  • Individualized care plans
